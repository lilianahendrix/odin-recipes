Twice Baked Potatoes

Description

This combination of potatoes, cheese, and bacon is a party favorite.

It's a great option for any special occassion and tastes amazing!

Ingredients

  • 10 lb bag of russet potatoes
  • 2 bars of butter, melted
  • 1/4 milk
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • Goya Adobo seasoning
  • Shredded Mexican cheese
  • Pack of bacon
  • Extra butter for greasing pan

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 400F. Cut potatoes in half.
  2. Put aluminum foil on sheet pan and add a thin layer of water and potatoes. Cover with aluminum foil and add holes to aluminum foil with knife.
  3. Cook for an hour or until potatoes feel tender. Continue adding water so the pan doesn't dry up. Stick a knife in the potatoes to see if they are tender.
  4. Let potatoes cool down. Then, scoop the inside of the potatoes out with a spoon into a bowl.
  5. Cover sheet pan with aluminum foil and grease with butter. Add the potato skins to prepared pan.
  6. Cook bacon to soft texture.
  7. In the bowl with potato filling, smash potatoes. Add melted butter, milk, sour cream, and adobo.
  8. Add smashed potatoes to the skins.
  9. Cut bacon into tiny pieces.
  10. Top with shredded cheese and bacon as desired.
  11. Cover with aluminum foil with holes. Bake at 400F for 30 minutes.
